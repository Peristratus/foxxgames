ATARI: Star Wars is a first-person rail shooter designed by Mike Hally and released in arcades by Atari, Inc. in 1983.It uses 3D color vector graphics to simulate the assault on the Death Star. Star Wars has been included on lists of the greatest video games of all time.

NINTENDO: Star Fox is a spaceship shooter game series.The games is about anthropomorphic animals called Star Fox, led by chief protagonist hero Fox McCloud. Gameplay involves adventures around the Lylat planetary system in the futuristic "Arwing" fighter aircraft.

SEGA: Street Fighter is a fighting video game franchise developed and published by Capcom.The player plays as martial artist Ryu, who competes in a worldwide martial arts tournament spanning five countries and 10 opponents. A second player can join in and plays as Ryu's American rival, Ken.
